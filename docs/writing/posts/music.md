# What Music Do You Listen To?

People often ask me what kind of music I listen to, and in my mind I almost never remember. Today I wanted to write a little bit more about the music I listen to and share some of my favorite albums with you. A lot of this has been co-written with the Spotify API, so everything is as true as it can be.

<!-- more -->

I think my couple of favorite artists are just [Chet Baker](https://open.spotify.com/artist/3rxeQlsv0Sc2nyYaZ5W71T), [Daniel Caesar](https://open.spotify.com/artist/20wkVLutqVOYrc0kxFs7rA), and [Mk.gee](https://open.spotify.com/artist/7tr9pbgNEKtG0GQTKe08Tz). There's a kind of longing that I really resonate with. Artists like [HYUKOH](https://open.spotify.com/artist/57okaLdCtv3nVBSn5otJkp) as well, but I also like to listen to a lot of guitar and ambient, trumpet and a bunch of other things.

---

## Ambient

### [Nala Sinephro](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2h5syT5XdsQgKLq8Yn1klO)

London harpist and composer. The first ambient artist I really got into — quieter than most and heavier than they look.

#### Space 1.8

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/51HFfu3GhuXa4VUnlpJJy8" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Long arcs of harp and synth that feel like watching light move through water — and then, without warning, something heavier comes through.

#### Endlessness

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/51CQQ3tQLRZlZJZ5jcpoGE" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

More orchestral, more patient. The title is accurate.

---

### [Floating Points](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2AR42Ur9PcchQDtEdwkv4L)

British musician and neuroscientist. Sounds equally at home in a warehouse and a concert hall.

#### Promises (with Pharoah Sanders & London Symphony Orchestra)

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/3ShtO5VCYa3ctlR5uzLWBa" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

One continuous piece across nine movements. Pharoah Sanders was 90 years old when this was recorded.

---

### [Leon Vynehall](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2o7L9DNcmzocYll1o0GGTU)

British electronic musician. Conceptual records with warmth that most electronic music doesn't bother with.

#### Nothing Is Still

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/6WeIO0CpDMiMXTglv0KuLr" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

A concept album about his grandparents emigrating to New York. *From The Sea/It Looms (Chapters I & II)* is one of my all-time favorite pieces of music.

---

### [92914](https://open.spotify.com/artist/0Zoe6ljAJo85rggnN6OaOF)

Japanese lo-fi ambient artist. Quiet guitar, soft percussion, found at 2am.

#### By The Calm Water

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/2iKcw2Cb04hYCibp30bc7U" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Where I started. *Night* is the track.

#### Home

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/5ZiXM0ggsayAFkzocedCev" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Companion record. *Someday* is the one.

#### Okinawa

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0vPsSVKMo07UijGL755gqL" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

The whole record sounds like being somewhere warmer than where you actually are.

## Classic Jazz

### [Chet Baker](https://open.spotify.com/artist/3rxeQlsv0Sc2nyYaZ5W71T)

American jazz trumpeter and vocalist, 1929–1988. My most-played artist — the reason I learned to play trumpet.

#### Chet Baker Sings

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/5JJ779nrbHx0KB2lBrMMa4" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Recorded 1954, still sounds like 3am.

#### I Can Dream, Can't I?

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/1Om5FVKUu8ymu92UHCMSpt" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

One of my most-played tracks. He knows exactly when not to sing.

---

### [Ryo Fukui](https://open.spotify.com/artist/4V7Ate3UISn8cy06xnAprh)

Japanese jazz pianist, 1948–2016. Self-taught, spent his whole life in Sapporo — nearly unknown outside Japan for decades.

#### Scenery

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/5Uny0mkKiVGDat7H6SNDyS" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Piano trio jazz recorded in 1976 that sounds like it was made in a snowstorm.

---

### [Frank Sinatra](https://open.spotify.com/artist/1Mxqyy3pSjf8kZZL4QVxS0)

American singer, 1915–1998. His phrasing is something every vocalist since has had to reckon with.

#### Nothing But The Best

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/3i67sGIVw8EBlgfSRv3Lj2" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

*Fly Me to the Moon* is the reason I'm here.

---

### [鈴木 弘 (Hiroshi Suzuki)](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2FatNL7pp4gwt10zTQHzbA)

Japanese jazz trombonist. His 1976 album *Cat* is a cult classic of Japanese jazz-funk.

#### キャット (Cat)

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/7DIXqjTX8IlgSKRR7DrhB9" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

One of the warmest-sounding records I know. *ロマンス* has one of the most immediately inviting opening bars of any album in my library.

---

## R&B

### [Daniel Caesar](https://open.spotify.com/artist/20wkVLutqVOYrc0kxFs7rA)

Canadian R&B singer-songwriter. Records about intimacy — the feeling of being close to someone and not quite being able to say what you mean.

#### Son Of Spergy

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/2LKW0m9cC63QzEI9tJH3ql" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

His latest. Every track does something interesting with space and silence.

#### NEVER ENOUGH

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0qGQrHicD7qXuz5VMlDuCe" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

More polished, more commercial — but *Ocho Rios* is one of his best songs.

#### H.E.R.

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0pV0Mx07aMApIpF19oSQgY" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Where he broke through. *Best Part* is still one of the most perfectly constructed R&B songs of the last decade.

---

### [Frank Ocean](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2h93pZq0e7k5yf4dywlkpM)

American singer-songwriter. Two studio albums in fifteen years, both landmarks.

#### Blonde

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/3mH6qwIy9crq0I9YQbOuDf" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

The only thing I listened to for my entire first four months in New York City.

#### Moon River

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0iqqnLXoocsMeCYlTw3Q2q" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

A cover, two minutes long. He does more with restraint here than most artists do with full albums.

---

### [daoud](https://open.spotify.com/artist/3e76yvk1gLZQhKZiUHkMsP)

Independent R&B artist. Minimal, raw — bedroom recordings that stayed bedroom recordings.

#### soda

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0M5GdlA2JQgZEuVeiHSnEm" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Lo-fi R&B that sounds like a text you weren't supposed to send.

---

### [YELLOW黃宣](https://open.spotify.com/artist/6iUs0Aijnu60VAAf8Aj2YM)

Taiwanese singer-songwriter. Between indie pop and experimental R&B — bilingual, doesn't fit neatly into any scene.

#### 翅膀和泥土

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/1J0iIYt7b1PBoniG4ZmYLg" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

The title track loops in my head for days.

---

## Indie Soul

### [Mk.gee](https://open.spotify.com/artist/7tr9pbgNEKtG0GQTKe08Tz)

American guitarist and songwriter. Incredible guitar — plays it the way Chet Baker plays trumpet, always aware of the silence around the notes. I have nine tracks from *Two Star* in my all-time top 50.

#### Two Star & The Dream Police

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/6DlLdXBGCsSDPOV8R2pCl7" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

His debut album. Put it on and let it run.

#### ROCKMAN

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/6IhX5HhXNXC8l0vv7QCrrK" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

A single. Three minutes of exactly what he does, distilled.

#### Fool

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/6TEN1TZhSRJQl1AfIDjR0y" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Earlier EP. The bones of *Two Star* are already here.

#### Lonely Fight

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/4tXUFRTPU3KhSBhAYwzWCW" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

One song. Plays like a closing argument.

---

### [Dijon](https://open.spotify.com/artist/0knGpCTbmG4ctl1wzYRZs4)

American singer-songwriter. R&B at the intersection of soul and classic country — confessional, not interested in being fashionable.

#### Baby

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/3hKlec1wgYVJcI0YvwCFJB" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

His debut album. *Baby!* opens the record and you know immediately what kind of artist he is.

#### Absolutely

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/4E691gbRgo2Zb6ToII2DWO" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

Rawer, more country-adjacent. *The Dress* and *Big Mike's* are two of his best.

---

### [Tom Misch](https://open.spotify.com/artist/1uiEZYehlNivdK3iQyAbye)

British guitarist and producer. Started from his bedroom — jazz guitar over hip-hop drums, looseness that's hard to manufacture.

#### Geography

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0hDnsNkxpMDZrpBlGjldtW" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

His debut album. Features De La Soul, Loyle Carner, GoldLink.

#### Six Songs

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/7KpjUEdi4nOl1MUhTHDzYF" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

The early EP. *Better Days* is the track.

---

### [FKJ](https://open.spotify.com/artist/6qqNVTkY8uBg9cP3Jd7DAH)

French multi-instrumentalist. Essentially live solo performances layered together — sounds effortless, took real craft.

#### French Kiwi Juice

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/0jJ7mMkCkTe7p9EJgSRxgi" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

His debut album. *Lying Together* and *Better Give U Up* are the ones to start with.

---

### [Masego](https://open.spotify.com/artist/2ow5cXN8oHK1KFkENaJ9ci)

American singer, saxophonist, and producer. Coined "TrapHouseJazz" — jazz harmony over trap production.

#### Lady Lady

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/2wqG59dVVQlJaxoO7N0LJt" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

His debut album. *Tadow* (with FKJ) became the gateway for a lot of people — including me.

---

### [G-DRAGON](https://open.spotify.com/artist/30b9WulBM8sFuBo17nNq9c)

Korean rapper, leader of BIGBANG. His solo records are weirder and more personal than the group work.

#### POWER

<iframe src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/3w2XYofMZpf1FA5wkkcBfx" width="100%" height="152" frameborder="0" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe>

I know. But one of the most purely fun records I've heard — maximalist, committed, completely uninterested in being cool about it.
